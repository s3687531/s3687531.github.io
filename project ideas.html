Motivation:

In the past ten years, the way of game operation has undergone fundamental changes. From the single way that game companies make profits by selling games, they have developed into a combination of games and peripheral services.

The increase of consumers and demand has become the decisive factor of game industry reform.On the analysis of the factors before, of course, take a look at August 13, 2019 update of the Top 100 Countries by Game Revenues ", the Top 10.

No form of entertainment can give players so much passion as games. They can not only actively participate, but also allow players of different emotions, lifestyles, interests, regions and income levels to enjoy the fun of games. The viewing experience is definitely one of the core elements, so almost any new game can be incorporated into a competitive mode that makes it a professional esports activity. There are also game anchors, professional gamers and professional teams with millions of fans.

So what's driving the boom in the game industry?

Reason 1: Material needs are fully met The increase in income has led to the satisfaction of people's basic material needs, and thus began to pursue spiritual satisfaction, the entire service industry has been developed, and of course the game industry is also.

Reason 2: The popularity of the universal platform reduces the threshold of the game Previously playing games required specialized equipment, so the game penetration rate was not high. The emergence of PCs and smart phones has effectively alleviated this problem, and the popularity of PCs and smartphones has enabled the game to spread effectively.

Reason 3: Experience to upgrade Game quality upgrade: from the earliest pixels to the current movie-level quality. Of course, the player is still on the 2D screen.

Higher dimensional experience upgrade: VR : It is a new generation of technology that everyone has high hopes for. This technology breaks the limitations of the screen experience and truly brings a new experience to a higher dimension. Such as: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qsna1ChGt0E

AR: The experience of AR is not the same as VR. VR is a completely immersive experience, but AR has created a new experience that can incorporate more elements of the real world. Such as: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uIHPPtPBgHk&t=69s

Reason 4: Social experience improvement: The previous games were not connected, but they could be played or co-operated at the same time. Everyone knows that two people are more interesting than one.

Current games such as League of Legends, CS:GO or World of Warcraft are multiplayer or multiplayer games. This also makes the game more interesting.

Reason 5: Job creation: As mentioned before, people are not satisfied with a single game experience, but still pursue these tangible things. Therefore, many new jobs have been born around the game: Cosplay, manual manufacturer of game characters, game anchor, game commentary and so on

The birth of these new jobs, while helping more people to find jobs, is also promoting the development of the game industry.

To sum up, the game industry is bound to get better and better, so I choose the game development project
